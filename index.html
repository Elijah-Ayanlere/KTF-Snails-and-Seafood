About KTF Snails and Seafoods

KTF Snails and Seafoods is a leading supplier of premium snails, seafood, and African staples. We pride ourselves on delivering fresh, sustainable, and delicious products to our customers. Our extensive range includes snails, shrimp, fish, crab, lobster, and other seafood delicacies, as well as African staples like fufu, garri, plantains, and yam.

Fresh Snails

Our fresh snails are meticulously sourced to maintain quality and flavor. They are perfect for creating authentic African delicacies and are a favorite among health-conscious individuals due to their high protein content and low fat.

Fresh Seafoods

From succulent shrimp to delectable fish, our fresh seafood options are unparalleled in taste and freshness. Whether for gourmet recipes or everyday meals, we ensure you get the finest catch every time.

African Staples

We bring you a rich selection of traditional African staples, including yam, cassava, plantains, and more. These essentials are perfect for creating hearty and nutritious meals that celebrate African culinary heritage.

Processed Products

Convenience meets quality in our range of processed products. From pre-cleaned snails to frozen seafood, we help save time while ensuring you still enjoy the same great taste and nutritional value.
